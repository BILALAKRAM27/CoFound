Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.

Last Updated: August 30, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing how you use our site, and personalizing content.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (which are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period of time).

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Authentication cookies to keep you logged in
  • Security cookies to protect against fraud
  • Session management cookies

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve the website's performance and user experience.

  • Analytics cookies to track page views and user behavior
  • Performance monitoring cookies
  • Error tracking cookies

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences, language settings, and customizations.

  • Language preference cookies
  • Theme and display preference cookies
  • User interface customization cookies

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement.

  • Advertising cookies for targeted marketing
  • Social media integration cookies
  • Retargeting cookies

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that we use to enhance your experience:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our website
  • Social Media Platforms: Enable social sharing and integration features
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Content Delivery Networks: Improve website loading speed and performance

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies on our website:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block only third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Cookie Consent Manager

We provide a cookie consent manager that allows you to:

  • Accept or decline different types of cookies
  • Update your preferences at any time
  • Withdraw consent for previously accepted cookies

Opt-Out Links

For third-party cookies, you can often opt out directly through the service provider:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Social Media: Check your account privacy settings

Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies, please be aware that doing so may affect your experience on our website:

  • Essential Functions: Some features may not work properly
  • User Experience: You may need to re-enter information repeatedly
  • Personalization: Content may not be tailored to your preferences
  • Analytics: We won't be able to improve our services based on usage data

We recommend allowing essential cookies to ensure the best possible experience.

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
