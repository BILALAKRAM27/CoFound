Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing how you use our platform, and personalizing content.

This Cookie Policy explains how CoFound uses cookies and similar technologies, what types of cookies we use, and how you can control them.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions.

  • Authentication: Remember your login status and session information
  • Security: Protect against fraud and maintain platform security
  • Functionality: Enable core platform features and services

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for platform operation.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Analytics: Track page views, user behavior, and platform performance
  • Error Monitoring: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Load Testing: Monitor platform speed and responsiveness

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

  • Language Preferences: Remember your preferred language
  • Theme Settings: Store your light/dark mode preference
  • Display Options: Remember your layout and view preferences
  • Form Data: Remember information you've entered in forms

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaign performance.

  • Advertising: Display relevant ads based on your interests
  • Campaign Tracking: Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns
  • Retargeting: Show ads to users who have visited our platform

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our platform are set by third-party services that we use to enhance functionality and analyze usage:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our platform
  • Facebook Pixel: Enables social media advertising and conversion tracking
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: Tracks professional networking and business interactions
  • Hotjar: Provides heatmaps and user behavior analytics

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your web browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Cookie Consent Manager

We provide a cookie consent manager that allows you to:

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Update your choices at any time
  • View detailed information about each cookie type

Opt-Out Options

For specific third-party services, you can opt out directly:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • LinkedIn: LinkedIn Cookie Settings

Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies, please note that doing so may affect your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Disabling these will prevent the platform from functioning properly
  • Functional Cookies: You may need to re-enter preferences each time you visit
  • Performance Cookies: We won't be able to identify and fix technical issues as quickly
  • Marketing Cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements

We recommend keeping essential and functional cookies enabled for the best user experience.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through our platform or email
  • Provide a summary of significant changes

Your continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
